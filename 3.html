Izzie

Izzie is the character of a webtoon Meow Man. Izzie goes to the same university as Bomi. Izzie is a puppy. Izzie works part-time at the same cafe as Chunbae. He doesn't approach Bomi and Chun-bae with good intentions at first, but he helps them later. And Izzie falls in love with Bomi. Recently, Chun-bae helped her confess.
